Character Guide
Rick and Morty Show

This show follows the misadventures of an alcholic scientist Rick and his nervous grandson Morty. If They aren't home with their family causing mayhem, then they are on one of their many intergalactic travel adventures doing so.

Understanding the Rick and Mory Universe

The world that these two characters occupy is apart of a multiverse. This mean there are an infinite number of universes with copies of themselves that exist in time, all of whom are different. However, in is some way this duo and all that exist of them seem to be the center of everything as seen through the creation the Citadel.
