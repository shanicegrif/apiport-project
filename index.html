RICKY AND MORTY CHARACTER UNIVERSE

Rick and Morty Show

This show follows the misadventures of an alcholic scientist Rick and his nervous grandson Morty. If They aren't home with their family causing mayhem, then they are on one of their many intergalactic travel adventures doing so.

Understanding the Rick and Mory Universe

The world that these two characters occupy is apart of a multiverse. This mean there are an infinite number of universes with copies of themselves that exist in time, all of whom are different. However, in is some way this duo and all that exist of them seem to be the center of everything as seen through the creation the Citadel. However, along the way they bump into many other characters that aren't themselves.

Rick Sanchez

A brilliant but boozy scientist, he needs some help on his many adventures so he needs a companion. He takes his grandson on his many adventures between different dimensions. Rick, known intergalactically and to the Council of Rick’s as Rick C-137, relating to the dimension that he lives in.

Morty Smith

Rick’s grandson, he is in high school and whilst trying to pass his classes (especially maths with Mr. Goldenfold) Rick is always taking him out of class to help him out on his adventures. Morty starts out being a lot more ethically moral than Rick, for example, he killed an assassin because he thinks human life should be protected. However, as the season goes on he becomes confident and charismatic, as well as becoming more like his grandfather.

Beth Sanchez

Rick’s daughter, he left her and his wife when she was a child and only came back 20 years later. Beth has abandonment issues due to this and she is terrified of her father leaving again. She is the father of Morty and Summer and the wife of Jerry.

Jerry Smith

The very scared husband of Beth, father of Morty and Summer, he is a very timid guy and is scared of almost everything. He doesn’t appreciate Rick after leaving Beth and for taking Morty on “high-tech sci-fi-rigmarole”.

Summer Smith

Morty’s sister and the daughter of Beth and Jerry, she always tries to prove herself as a good companion to Rick so she’ll be invited more but is actually quite an emotional teenager at heart. She cried when the Devil fired her from the business she helped him create.
